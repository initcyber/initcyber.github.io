<div class="interior-section">
  <h2>Appeals Process</h2>
  <p>Init Cyber provides a formal appeals process for clients who wish to contest a decision made in connection with their engagement, including matters related to deliverable acceptance, scope disputes, or contract interpretation.</p>
</div>

<div class="interior-section">
  <h3>Grounds for Appeal</h3>
  <ul class="interior-list">
    <li>Disagreement with a formal deliverable rejection or acceptance decision</li>
    <li>Disputes regarding scope of work or statement of work interpretation</li>
    <li>Concerns about billing or invoicing accuracy</li>
    <li>Disputes arising from a complaint resolution decision</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="interior-section">
  <h3>How to Submit an Appeal</h3>
  <p>Appeals must be submitted in writing within <strong>30 calendar days</strong> of the decision being appealed. Submit to:</p>
  <p><strong><a href="mailto:justin@initcyber.com">justin@initcyber.com</a></strong><br>
  Attn: Justin Johnson, CEO/Owner — Appeal Request</p>
  <p>Your appeal submission must include:</p>
  <ul class="interior-list">
    <li>Reference to the original decision or complaint case number (if applicable)</li>
    <li>A clear statement of the grounds for appeal</li>
    <li>Any new evidence or documentation not previously considered</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="interior-section">
  <h3>Review and Decision</h3>
  <p>Appeals are reviewed by Init Cyber's senior leadership within <strong>20 business days</strong>. The decision rendered on appeal is final at the company level. Unresolved disputes may be subject to the dispute resolution provisions outlined in your contract or applicable federal acquisition regulations.</p>
</div>
