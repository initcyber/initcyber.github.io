  <h2>We're Building the Next Generation of Defense Cybersecurity</h2>

  <ul class="interior-list">

    <li>Experience with NIST SP 800-171, CMMC, RMF, or FedRAMP frameworks</li>
    <li>Technical hands-on capability — scripting, cloud platforms, vulnerability tools</li>
    <li>Strong documentation skills for SSPs, POA&amp;Ms, and audit evidence</li>
    <li>CISSP, Security+, CISM, or equivalent certifications a plus</li>
  </ul>
