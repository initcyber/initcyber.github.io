<div class="interior-section">
  <h2>Work That Actually Matters</h2>
  <p>Init Cyber is a Cyber-AB authorized C3PAO built around one conviction: the defense supply chain deserves better than checkbox compliance. We're a small, focused team doing serious work: official CMMC assessments and advisory engagements that directly affect the security posture of organizations supporting the warfighter. If that mission resonates with you, we want to hear from you.</p>
</div>

<div class="interior-section">
  <h3>What We Look For</h3>
  <ul class="interior-list">
    <li>CMMC credentials: LCCA, CCA, CCP</li>
    <li>Deep familiarity with NIST SP 800-171, the CMMC Assessment Guide, and DFARS requirements</li>
    <li>Strong assessment and documentation skills including SSPs, POA&amp;Ms, evidence packages, and findings reports</li>
    <li>Ability to communicate technical findings clearly to non-technical stakeholders</li>
    <li>On the flip side, practical technical depth across cloud platforms, operating systems, and network architecture... no two client environments look the same.
    <li>CISSP, Security+, or equivalent certifications a plus</li>
    <li>Prior DoD, defense contracting, or federal cybersecurity experience preferred</li>
  </ul>
</div>
