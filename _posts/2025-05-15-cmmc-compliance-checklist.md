Preparing for a CMMC assessment can feel overwhelming — 110 practices, mountains of documentation, and high stakes for your DoD contracts. This checklist distills the most critical preparation steps into a manageable action list.

## Before the Assessment

### Documentation Essentials

- [ ] **System Security Plan (SSP)** — A complete, up-to-date SSP documenting your CUI environment
- [ ] **Plan of Action & Milestones (POA&M)** — Tracking any open gaps with realistic closure dates
- [ ] **Network diagrams** — Current diagrams showing all in-scope systems and data flows
- [ ] **Asset inventory** — A complete list of hardware and software in your CUI environment
- [ ] **User access list** — Current list of all users with access to CUI systems

### Technical Controls

- [ ] Multi-factor authentication (MFA) enabled for all CUI system access
- [ ] Encryption at rest and in transit for all CUI
- [ ] Endpoint detection and response (EDR) deployed on all in-scope systems
- [ ] Patch management process documented and current
- [ ] Log collection and retention in place (minimum 90 days)
- [ ] Incident response plan tested within the past year

### Process and Policy

- [ ] Security awareness training completed by all personnel with CUI access
- [ ] Background screening completed for all relevant personnel
- [ ] Acceptable use policy acknowledged by all users
- [ ] Configuration management process documented
- [ ] Change management records maintained

## Common Gaps We See

In our experience assessing defense contractors, these are the most frequently missed requirements:

**Access Control**: Many organizations struggle with least-privilege enforcement, particularly for shared accounts and privileged users.

**Audit and Accountability**: Log collection is often incomplete, missing network devices or cloud services that touch CUI.

**Configuration Management**: Baseline configurations are frequently undocumented or outdated.

**Incident Response**: IR plans exist on paper but haven't been exercised, which assessors will probe.
