<div class="interior-section">
  <h2>Complaint Policy</h2>
  <p>Init Cyber is committed to providing professional, high-quality cybersecurity consulting services. If you have a concern about the conduct of our staff, the quality of services rendered, or any aspect of your engagement with Init Cyber, we want to hear from you.</p>
</div>

<div class="interior-section">
  <h3>How to Submit a Complaint</h3>
  <p>Complaints may be submitted in writing via email to:</p>
  <p><strong><a href="mailto:justin@initcyber.com">justin@initcyber.com</a></strong><br>
  Attn: Justin Johnson, CEO/Owner</p>
  <p>Please include the following in your complaint:</p>
  <ul class="interior-list">
    <li>Your name, organization, and contact information</li>
    <li>A clear description of the concern or issue</li>
    <li>The date(s) the issue occurred</li>
    <li>Any supporting documentation or correspondence</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="interior-section">
  <h3>Resolution Process</h3>
  <p>All complaints are acknowledged within <strong>3 business days</strong> of receipt. We aim to provide a full written response and proposed resolution within <strong>15 business days</strong>. If additional time is required to investigate, you will be notified with an updated timeline.</p>
</div>

<div class="interior-section">
  <h3>Escalation</h3>
  <p>If you are not satisfied with the resolution provided, you may escalate your complaint to the appropriate federal contracting officer or oversight body associated with your contract vehicle.</p>
</div>
