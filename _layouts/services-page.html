How We Work

Our Proven Process

01

Discovery

We define your CUI boundary, inventory all in-scope assets, and run a quantitative gap analysis against all 110 NIST 800-171 practices to produce your baseline SPRS score.

02

Hardening

We execute a risk-prioritized remediation plan — closing control gaps, authoring your SSP and POA&M, and hardening your environment to withstand a C3PAO assessment.

03

Automation

We implement automated evidence collection, continuous compliance monitoring, and alert workflows so your posture is maintained without manual effort between assessments.

04

Governance

We establish the policies, training cadences, and review cycles that keep your program audit-ready as the threat landscape and CMMC requirements evolve.
