The 'Start Secure' Philosophy

Init Cyber was founded on a straightforward conviction: the defense supply chain deserves better than checkbox compliance. We watched contractors struggle under the weight of CMMC requirements with no clear guidance, and we watched adversaries exploit those gaps. That's why we built a firm focused on one thing — doing this right.

Start Secure isn't a marketing tagline. It's the standard we hold every engagement to. Whether we're conducting an official CMMC assessment or advising an organization on their path to certification, the goal is always the same: accurate results, honest findings, and a defensible security posture.
