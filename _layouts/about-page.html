Our Story

Our Journey: Building Trust in Cybersecurity

Init Cyber was founded by former Department of Defense cybersecurity professionals who saw a critical gap: defense contractors needed genuine CMMC expertise, not generic compliance advice.

We built our practice around one goal — making CMMC compliance accessible, understandable, and achievable for every organization in the defense industrial base, regardless of size.

Today, we've guided dozens of organizations through successful CMMC certifications, earning a reputation for straight talk, deep expertise, and real results.

Our Core Values

Mathematical Precision

Every control gap, risk score, and remediation priority is quantified — not estimated. We bring data discipline to every engagement.

Regulatory Rigor

We hold ourselves to the same standard assessors do. No shortcuts, no scope carve-outs, no compliance theater.

Absolute Transparency

You will always know exactly where you stand — gaps, risks, timelines, and costs — presented plainly without spin.

Adaptive Vigilance

The threat landscape and the CMMC ruleset both evolve. Our programs are designed to adapt so your certification doesn't expire with the regulations.
