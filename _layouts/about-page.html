Our Story

Our Journey: Building Trust in Cybersecurity

Init Cyber was founded by former Department of Defense cybersecurity professionals who saw a critical gap: defense contractors needed genuine CMMC expertise, not generic compliance advice.

We built our practice around one goal — making CMMC compliance accessible, understandable, and achievable for every organization in the defense industrial base, regardless of size.

Today, we've guided dozens of organizations through successful CMMC certifications, earning a reputation for straight talk, deep expertise, and real results.
