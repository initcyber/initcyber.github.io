Capability Statement

Init Cyber is committed to delivering cutting-edge cybersecurity and IT solutions, leveraging a wealth of experience in both government and commercial sectors. Our expertise in managing complex compliance requirements and optimizing cloud security positions us as a valuable partner for organizations seeking to enhance their cybersecurity posture and IT infrastructure.

Init Cyber is a Cyber-AB authorized Third-Party Assessment Organization (C3PAO) delivering official CMMC certification assessments and advisory services for the Defense Industrial Base. Led by a Lead Certified CMMC Assessor (LCCA), Init Cyber brings technical precision and regulatory expertise to every engagement. We maintain strict conflict-of-interest separation between assessment and advisory clients in full compliance with CMMC program requirements, so our findings are always objective, independent, and DoD-accepted.
